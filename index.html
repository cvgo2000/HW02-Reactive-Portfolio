About Me

Hello! My name is Christian Go! I was born and raised in San Diego, California. I've lived in Colorado since exiting military service with the USMC back in 2001 and have been loving it since! While California is my birth state and will always have a special place in my heart, I just love the weather variety Colorado offers.

I currently work in the medical insurance field as a contractor to Anthem Blue Cross Blue Shield, which I've done for the last 10 years. The decision to get into coding has been a somewhat spontaneous move as it is a field I've at the very least had a passing interest in. I've got several friends in this line of work who encouraged me to follow this path, so here I am!

I like to consider myself a weird mixture of traveller and homebody. I've visited locales across the United States as well as Japan, the Philippines, China and Mexico, but would like to visit Austrailia and Europe at some point in my life. My day to day hobbies include reading, video games, watching movies and taking simple pleasure in sleeping. Thanks for taking the time to visit!
